A WhatsApp for Global Finance.

We are building the decentralized financial infrastructure for the borderless economy. A platform where sending $10,000 from New York to a freelancer in Bangalore takes two clicks, is instantly settled on Layer-2 blockchain rails, and costs practically zero.

The Thesis

Cryptocurrency's original promise was to act as Peer-to-Peer electronic cash. Yet, complexity has alienated the masses. By abstracting away the complex cryptography and tokenomics, we deliver the promise of Web3 to everyday users directly through the simplicity of an `@username` handle.

Compliant Backbone

Zing operates strictly within legal frameworks. We partner with regulated entities to automatically off-ramp stablecoins into fiat like INR natively, completely handling FEMA KYC and AML in the background.
